About FestivaLearn

FestivaLearn is designed to help users, especially the younger generation, reconnect with cultural traditions and understand the science behind various festivals.

Our Goals

  • Preserve the knowledge of traditional festivals.
  • Provide daily or periodic reminders of important cultural days.
  • Educate users on the scientific and cultural significance of festivals.
  • Encourage participation and learning through interactive features.

Why This Matters

Many festivals are gradually forgotten, especially among Gen Z. FestivaLearn ensures that users not only remember the dates but also understand the meaning, rituals, and scientific reasons behind each festival.
